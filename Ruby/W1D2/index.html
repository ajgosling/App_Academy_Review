Shakshuka

Description

Shakshouka (Arabic: شكشوكة‎, also spelled shakshuka or chakchouka) is a dish of eggs poached in a sauce of tomatoes, olive oil, peppers, onion and garlic, and commonly spiced with cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper and nutmeg. The dish has existed in Mediterranean cultures for centuries. Shakshouka

Ingredients

    Olive oil Onion Bell pepper Chopped Tomatoes Ground Cumin Garlic
